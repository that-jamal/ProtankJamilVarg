Protank Oy Ab

The transport company Protank Ab was founded in 2010.

At that time, assignments were carried out with a single tanker truck for mostly one customer.

Protank specializes in the transport of liquid bulk products.

Our services include the transport of hazardous materials for the industry.

Our trained professional drivers ensure that chemicals are transported in a safe and secure manner. All our drivers have ADR driving permits.

Through regular training, we keep our drivers and the company up to date.

We are proud to work for companies such as UPM, Boliden, and Tetra Chemicals Europe.

Our business is built on a close relationship with the customer, flexibility, modern equipment, and professional drivers.

Flexibility & Precision

At Protank, we work systematically, both in internal company matters and in our interactions with clients.

Our greatest strength is flexibility and our ability to offer the highest possible quality in liquid product tanker transport.

Good cooperation with the customer is particularly important to us.

Our clients can trust us and confidently leave the transportation and related details in our hands.

Most of our assignments are contract transports and always involve some form of scheduling.

Our transport planner ensures that each delivery meets the client's expectations.

Quality & Safety

The Protank staff operates responsibly according to our core values, with a focus on proactive safety measures.

Everyone is obligated to report any deficiencies or hazards observed in the work environment or operations.

Any activity that endangers safety must be addressed immediately.

We carry out our transport tasks whenever possible between 6:00 AM and 12:00 AM.

By doing so, we ensure the safety of the transportation and that our drivers do not have to work while fatigued.

We also ensure the safety of our drivers during loading and unloading by providing them with all necessary personal protective equipment.

Of course, we adhere to all industry-related safety requirements.

Each driver has a significant responsibility for cleaning the tank and other equipment after unloading, and they follow the work routines we have established ourselves.

We do this because this is a critical moment in the handling of our clients' products, requiring experience and expertise to ensure that everything proceeds smoothly and without the need for complaints.

Vehicles

All our tankers are approved for transporting ADR-classified products in classes 3, 5, 6, 8, and 9.

When constructing new tanks, we focus on making them as easy to clean and inspect as possible.

We also have our own requirements for the types of adapters that must be present on the vehicle, ensuring that we can always connect our tankers to the client's tanks.

Therefore, we have most possible connections available in two- and three-inch sizes.

Our vehicles are also equipped with compressors for unloading.

Whenever we purchase a new vehicle, we choose the highest environmental classifications.

As a result, all our vehicles have Euro 5 and 6 environmental ratings.
